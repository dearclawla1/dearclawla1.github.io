Mouse-Key Mapper

Map mouse clicks to keyboard keys for automation and productivity

How to Use

  1. Click "Start Listening" to enable click detection
  2. Click anywhere on the page to map that location to a key
  3. Choose a keyboard key from the dropdown (or press a physical key)
  4. Click "Save Mapping" to store the mapping
  5. Click "Load Mappings" to restore saved mappings
